Insurance Information

Our office gladly accepts most major PPO plans including but not limited to the following: Metlife, Aetna, Cigna, Delta, Guardian, United Healthcare, United Concordia, Sun Life…

Dental insurances are generally great for diagnostic & preventive care, but many will have a yearly maximum and will oftentimes not cover what may be in the patient’s’ best interest. Here we practice comprehensive care and look at each patient not just as a set of teeth but as an individual. We try and tailor treatment options that will best benefit each person, and unfortunately more often than not those do not encompass what insurances will cover.

Insurance plans have their own agenda that aren’t always indicated for each individual and we always discuss that in the our office. We’ll be happy to help you understand your insurance coverage better and work with or around it in providing you excellent and customized patient care. For more information: Insurance & Payments (Note: No Medicaid or community dental plans accepted)
